# 👋 About Me

Hi, I am **Junyang Chen (陈俊扬).** I am currently a final-year undergraduate student at South China Normal University, majoring in Software Engineering, advised by Prof. Huan Yang. I will soon begin my M.Phil. studies at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), advised by <a href="https://chenjb1997.github.io/">Prof. Jingbang Chen</a>.

My research interests center on Large Language Models (LLMs), with a particular focus on efficient training and inference acceleration. I am also broadly interested in retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), AI agents, recommender systems, and explainable AI (XAI).
